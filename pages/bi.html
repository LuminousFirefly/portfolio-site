Bleeding In

We bled,
Bled out all we desire,
Bled out everything we were,
And called it therapy.

Draining our fallible bodies,
Of the vitality they yearned for,
Of the dreams they stubbornly clung to,
And called it a cleansing.

We bled,
Tearing apart the skin that bound us,
Clawing out parts of the wayfaring stranger,
I once called me.

You bled.
With violence so magnificent,
Torture so relentlessly beautiful,

You bled into me.

Aryan Raj Dhawan
